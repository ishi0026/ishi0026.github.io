About Me
I am from Japan. Before I came to Canada, I had been working in the university. I am really interested in educational field. When I was a university student, I did internship in prep school for high school students. I supported them to find way to study and sometimes taught them English. Also, I majored “Body Expression and Cinematic Arts”, it was kind of art field. I acted, took photos, wrote scripts and shoot video. After graduation, I forgot all about being creative, but I am happy to try again with this program. I am nervous about this new challenge, but I will do my best based on the principle of doing what looks interesting to me.
My hobbies include listening to music. I am a big fan of The Beatles. This is because I played their song in music band club when I was a high school student. In addition, my mother also likes them. The Beatles songs are able to hear in Canada, so when I listen them, it reminds me of my younger day’s memory. Also, I like to read books. Recently, I am reading “The Culture Map”. It is about the difference working place culture around the world.
